Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

AutoPub collects account information, authentication provider data, payment and subscription data through Creem, extension usage events, AI task inputs and outputs, and waitlist entries. We do not collect browsing history or page content beyond what you explicitly scan, audit, or capture through an extension action.

How We Use Information

We use your information to authenticate you, process payments, provision credits, deliver AI results, improve the product, and communicate product updates, billing notices, and waitlist announcements. We do not sell personal data.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored in Supabase with row-level security. Payment processing is handled by Creem; AutoPub does not store full payment credentials. Server-only secrets, API keys, and webhook secrets are never shipped to browser extensions or frontend code.

Third-Party Services

AutoPub relies on Supabase, Creem, Vercel, and AI model providers for product operation. Each provider has its own privacy and security practices.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the Support page.
