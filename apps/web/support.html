Support Paths

Tell us what you are trying to fix

The fastest support request explains the product area, what happened, and what you expected instead.

Extension support

For Visual Saver, Video Analyzer, SEO Auditor, or Competitor Monitor issues.

  • Extension name and version
  • Page URL you analyzed
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshot or error message

Account and credits

For login, plan, credit refresh, AI action, or entitlement problems.

  • Your account email
  • Your plan: Free, Pro, or Pro Plus
  • AI task type and approximate time
  • What the credit balance showed

Billing and SaaS waitlist

For Creem checkout, subscription, cancellation, or V2 SaaS access questions.

  • Plan or waitlist tier
  • Approximate transaction date
  • Checkout or portal message
  • Whether the question is V1 or V2
Before You Reach Out

Quick checks

Is Chrome up to date?

Use the latest Chrome version and install AutoPub extensions from the Chrome Web Store release package only.

Are you signed in?

AI credits, plan limits, and paid entitlements require a registered account session.

Is the page allowed?

AutoPub supports your own pages, public pages, or content you are authorized to access. It does not support private or protected content scraping.
