AutoPub SaaS - V2 Waitlist

The final product: help your website publish content that brings customers.

The extensions solve manual growth work first. The SaaS is the larger vision: understand your website, plan topics, write SEO-friendly articles, generate images, publish content, and keep optimizing based on performance. In V1, this section is for product display, early access reservations, and demand validation only.

Auto Plan

Analyzes your website, product catalog, and target audience. Maps Google search intent, customer questions, and competitor content gaps — then builds a prioritized editorial calendar aligned with your exact business scope.

Auto Create

Generates articles optimized for Google ranking — with proper heading hierarchy, structured data, internal links to your product and service pages, and content authority signals. Every article aligns with your website's topic scope, product catalog, and brand voice. Built for both search engine crawlers and AI answer engines like SGE and ChatGPT.

Auto Illustrate

Creates or selects images that reinforce article topics and your brand identity — product visuals, explanatory infographics, data comparison charts, and SEO-friendly images with proper alt text, file names, and compression for fast page load.

Auto Optimize

Tracks Google rankings, click-through rates, AI search visibility, and competitor content changes. Automatically refreshes published articles with updated data, stronger internal linking, and improved topical depth — so your content keeps earning traffic instead of decaying.

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about AutoPub extensions and the SaaS platform.

Chrome Extensions

Do the extensions work together?

Each extension is independent — install one, two, or all four. They share the same account and credit system, so a Pro subscription unlocks AI features across every extension you install.

How does the Visual Saver work?

Open any webpage, click the Visual Saver extension, and hit Scan. It finds every visible image and CSS background, de-duplicates them, and shows dimensions. Pro users can batch download and run AI to reverse-engineer image prompts.

How does the SEO Auditor work?

Click Audit on any page. The extension checks title length, meta description, H1 count, image alt text, canonical URLs, and more — scored instantly. Pro users get AI fix suggestions and can copy the full report.

Account & Credits

Do I get free credits when I register?

Yes. When you create a free account, you receive 3 credits every day to try AI features. No credit card required. Each day your credits refresh — use them or lose them.

Do anonymous users get credits?

Anonymous visitors get 3 one-time trial credits to test AI features. Once used, you must register to receive daily credit refresh. Registering takes 30 seconds.

What happens when I upgrade?

Upgrading to Pro or Pro Plus replaces your daily credit model with a monthly allowance. Unused daily credits do not roll over. Your new billing cycle starts immediately.

AutoPub SaaS

How is the SaaS different from the extensions?

The extensions are tools your team clicks to use. The SaaS runs autonomously on a schedule: it plans topics, writes articles, generates images, publishes to your site, and optimizes over time — with your approval, or fully hands-off once you trust it.

Does Pro Plus include the SaaS?

No. Extension plans cover the Chrome extensions only. The SaaS is a separate V2 product with its own pricing. Extension subscribers get priority beta access.
