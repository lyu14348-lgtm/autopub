Frequently asked questions

Find answers about the extensions, the SaaS platform, and your account.

Chrome Extensions

Do the extensions work together?

Each extension is independent — you can install one, two, or all four. They share the same account and credit system, so a Pro subscription unlocks AI features across every extension you install.

How does the Visual Saver work?

Open any webpage, click the Visual Saver extension, and hit Scan. It finds every visible image and CSS background on the page, de-duplicates them, and shows dimensions. Pro users can batch download and run AI to reverse-engineer image generation prompts.

How does the Video Analyzer work?

Visit a page with a video or paste a public video URL. The analyzer detects video metadata. Pro users run AI analysis to break down hooks, pacing, selling points, emotional triggers, and CTAs — so your team understands why a creative performs.

How does the SEO Auditor work?

Click Audit on any page. The extension checks title length, meta description, H1 count, image alt text, canonical URLs, and more. You get a score and issue list instantly. Pro users get AI-generated fix suggestions and can copy the full report.

How does the Competitor Monitor work?

Visit a competitor page, click Capture to save a snapshot of titles, pricing, CTAs, and H1s. Later, return to the same page and click Compare to see what changed. Pro users get AI summaries explaining likely strategy shifts.

What pages can I analyze?

You can analyze any page you have legitimate access to — your own site, publicly available pages, or authorized content. AutoPub does not support scraping private, protected, or restricted content.

AutoPub SaaS

What is the AutoPub SaaS?

It is a standalone platform (V2) that reads your website, plans what content to create, writes SEO-optimized articles with matching images, publishes them, and continuously optimizes based on performance. The extensions are manual tools for today; the SaaS is the automated content engine for tomorrow.

How is the SaaS different from the extensions?

The extensions are tools your team clicks to use — you open a page, run an analysis, get results. The SaaS runs autonomously on a schedule: it plans topics, writes articles, generates images, publishes to your site, and optimizes over time — with your approval at each stage, or fully hands-off once you trust it.

When is the SaaS launching?

We are collecting signals through extension usage, paid conversion, and waitlist demand. If V1 signals are strong, private beta starts within 6-12 weeks. Join the waitlist on the homepage to lock in founder pricing.

Does Pro Plus include the SaaS?

No. Extension plans (Free, Pro, Pro Plus) cover the Chrome extensions only. The SaaS is a separate V2 product with its own pricing. Extension subscribers get priority access to the SaaS beta.

Can the SaaS run completely on autopilot?

Yes. You start with review and approval at each stage. Once you are comfortable with the quality and consistency, switch to full automation — the platform runs hands-off, freeing you to focus on strategy while it handles content operations on complete autopilot.

Account & Billing

Do I get free credits when I register?

Yes. When you create a free account, you receive 3 credits every day to try AI-powered features across all four extensions. No credit card is required. Anonymous visitors get basic features only; registering unlocks daily credits, session saving, and the ability to upgrade.

What happens to my credits when I upgrade?

When you upgrade from Free to Pro or Pro Plus, your plan's credit allowance replaces the Free tier's credits. Unused credits from your previous plan do not roll over. Your new billing cycle starts immediately, and your new credit amount is available right away.

Can I switch plans mid-month?

Yes. When you upgrade mid-cycle, the new plan takes effect immediately and your billing is prorated. When you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through Creem. You can cancel from your customer portal at any time. Your Pro or Pro Plus features remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan.

What happens if I run out of credits?

AI-powered features become unavailable until your next credit refresh. Free credits refresh daily; Pro and Pro Plus credits follow the monthly billing cycle. Basic features - scanning, auditing, and capturing - continue to work without interruption.

Is there a refund policy?

Refund policies follow the terms presented at checkout through Creem. If you have a billing issue, contact support with your plan details and the transaction date. Pro Plus subscribers receive priority billing support.

Still have questions?

Our support team is ready to help. Include your extension name, page URL, and plan when you reach out.
